Clinic Details

Before deciding to make an appointment please read the following carefully!

  • This practice accepts consultation request in regards to any age group.
  • Individual, couple, family therapy and/or group therapy is offered/
  • I specialise in ASC (Asperger/HFA) and similar presentations.
  • Consultations are generally available Tuesdays to Fridays.
  • Consultations are scheduled 55 minutes in duration.
  • Consultation fees are $185.00 per hour.
  • Group consultations are $50.00 per hour.
  • Consultations on behalf of an agency are charged at the full Australian Psychological Society rate of $218.00 per hour.
  • Payment is due at the time of each session unless other arrangements have been negotiated.
  • No credit card or EFTPOS facilities are available yet (in progress), internet banking, cheque or cash accepted.
  • Cancellation or re-booking requires 48 hour notice.
  • Non-attendance and late cancellations incur full charges if the time slot can not be re-allocated.
  • Skype sessions, Telephone consults, Email consults and Consultation extensions are charged at $40.00 per 15 minute intervals.
  • Travel Time fee is charged for out-of-office sessions.
  • Medicare offers a rebate for psychological treatment after a mental health examination and referral from your GP.
  • Medicare explained; please check here.
  • Most Health Funds provide benefits for Psychological Consultations. Please enquire with your health fund.

When you have decided to book a session please complete the Registration Form and bring it along to the first session
or email it to me at info@jdpsy.org.
