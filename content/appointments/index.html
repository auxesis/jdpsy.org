Jeroen specialises in psychological therapies for people on the Autism Spectrum. The focus of treatment is life skills and strategies for managing day to day situations. Jeroen likes to include family in the therapeutic process. Partners, family and or close friends are encouraged to attend the first session at least.

Bookings are handled by Jeroen’s Administrative assistant Julia.

Julia works part time as Jeroen’s Administrative assistant. She has a Bachelor of Nursing, Graduate Certificate in Human Services and is studying a Graduate Diploma of Psychology.

Session information

Before requesting an appointment please read this carefully!

Payment can be made via bank transfer or Medipass. - Cancellation or rescheduling requires 48 hour notice. - Non-attendance and late cancellations may incur full charges. - Medicare explained – [please read more](https://jdpsy.org/resources/patients2.pdf). - Most Health Funds provide benefits for Psychological Consultations. How to make an appointment

To ensure we comply with COVIDSafe protocols most of Jeroen’s sessions are held online via Zoom Pro. Since the introduction of Telehealth Jeroen now offers sessions to clients Australia wide.

Face masks must be worn during face-to-face sessions. The main benefit of online sessions is no facemasks.

{{% md %}} ## Referrals **You do NOT need a referral to see a Psychologist.** {{% /md %}}

However, with an appropriate referral you may be able to claim a Medicare rebate under the Better Access Initiative. The Better Access Initiative allows for 10 (6+4) sessions. Another 10 COVID sessions were made available by the government in 2020.

{{% md %}} In order for Medicare to process your claim the referral letter must have the following: * Patient’s name, address, date of birth * Mental health diagnosis - for example anxiety, depression. * Addressed to a named Psychologist or ‘The Psychologist’ * GP referrals only: Make mention of or include a Mental Health Care Plan. * Number of appointments requested. ## Telehealth Jeroen’s preference is Telehealth consultations. The benefit of telehealth is that you can talk to Jeroen from the comfort of your own home without the need for a facemask. ## COVIDSafe face to face sessions {{% /md %}} {{% md %}} Face to face sessions are only available on a limited basis.
