Jeroen specialises in psychological therapies for Autistic adults. The focus of treatment is life skills and strategies for managing day to day situations. Jeroen likes to include family in the therapeutic process. Partners, family and or close friends are encouraged to attend at least the first session.

Bookings are handled by Jeroen’s clinic assistant Julia.

Julia works part time as Jeroen’s clinic assistant. She has a Bachelor of Nursing, Graduate Certificate in Human Services and is studying a Graduate Diploma of Psychology.

Session information

Before requesting an appointment please read this carefully!
{{% md %}} - Consultations are generally available Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. 8am-4pm. - Consultations run for 60 minutes - Initial consultations run for 90 minutes - Consultation fees are $220.00 (60 minutes). - Initial Consultation fees: $250.00 (up to 90 minutes, including pre-reading). - Group consultations are $55.00 per hour (no Medicare rebate is available for Telehealth group sessions). - Consultations on behalf of any agency are charged at the Australian Psychological Society recommended rate of $267.00 per hour. - Payment is due at the time of each session unless other arrangements have been negotiated. - EFTPOS facilities are available, cheques or cash are accepted. - Telehealth sessions will receive an invoice via email. Payment can be made via bank transfer or Medipass. - Cancellation or rescheduling requires 48 hour notice. - Non-attendance and late cancellations may incur full charges. - Medicare explained – [please read more](/resources/patients2.pdf). - Most Health Funds provide benefits for Psychological Consultations. Please enquire with your health fund. {{% /md %}}
How to make an appointment

Please email us at info@jdpsy.org and Julia will reply to arrange an appointment.

Please briefly describe:

  • Name of prospective client
  • Age of prospective client
  • What you're looking for
  • Who you have been referred by (optional)

{{% md %}} ## Referrals **You do NOT need a referral to see a Psychologist.** {{% /md %}}

However, with an appropriate referral you may be able to claim a Medicare rebate under the Better Access Initiative. The Better Access Initiative allows for 10 (6+4) sessions. Another 10 COVID sessions were made available by the government in 2020.

{{% md %}} ## Telehealth only Jeroen only offers Telehealth consultations, via Zoom Pro. {{% /md %}}
