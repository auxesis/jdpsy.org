Jeroen specialises in psychological therapies for Autistic adults. The focus of treatment is life skills and strategies for managing day to day situations. Jeroen likes to include family in the therapeutic process. Partners, family and or close friends are encouraged to attend at least the first session.

Bookings are handled by Jeroen’s therapy assistant Julia.

Julia works part time as Jeroen’s therapy assistant. She has a Bachelor of Nursing, Graduate Certificate in Human Services and a Graduate Diploma of Psychology.

Session information

Before requesting an appointment please read this carefully!
{{% md %}} * Consultations are generally available Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. 8am-4pm. * JDPSY fees * Standard consultation is $240 (up to 60 minutes) * Initial consultation is $270. * Group consultations are $60.00 per 90 min session. * Payment is due at the time of each session. * Card payment is preferred, payment request is sent via Tyro Health. * Cancellation or rescheduling requires 48 hour notice. * Non-attendance and late cancellations incur charges. * Medicare explained – please read more. * NDIS participants need to be self-managed or plan managed. We are not an NDIA registered provider. {{% /md %}}
How to make an appointment

Please email us at info@jdpsy.org and Julia will reply to arrange an appointment.

Please briefly describe:

  • Name of prospective client
  • Age of prospective client
  • What you're looking for
  • Who you have been referred by (optional)

{{% md %}} ## Referrals **You do NOT need a referral to see a Psychologist.** {{% /md %}}

However, with an appropriate referral you may be able to claim a Medicare rebate under the Better Access Initiative. The Better Access Initiative allows for 10 (6+4) sessions.

{{% md %}} ## Telehealth only Jeroen only offers Telehealth consultations, via Zoom Pro. {{% /md %}}
