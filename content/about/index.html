{{% md %}} ## About Jeroen Decates Jeroen is a Clinical Psychologist and earned a Bachelor of Psychology (BPsych) and a Masters in Social Sciences (MSocSci) in the Netherlands at the University of Nijmegen. Jeroen has 45 years experience in Psychology and has been living and working in Australia since 1982. Jeroen was previously a Senior Clinical Psychologist at Hornsby Ku-ring-gai Health Services for 28 years and has been in private practice since 1983. {{% /md %}}
