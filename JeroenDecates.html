About Jeroen Decates

I am a Clinical Psychologist with a Bachelors Degree in Psychology (BPsych) and a Masters Degree in Social Sciences (MSocSci) from the University of Nijmegen in the Netherlands. Practicing Psychology started in the Netherlands in 1976 and I moved to Australia in 1982. When I am not at this clinic I work for the Hornsby Ku-ring-gai Health Services in the position of Senior Clinical Psychologist.
Papers on a range of topics have been presented at National and International Conferences and at School, Church and Community Groups. Interviews on a range of psychological issues have taken place for Television, Radio and Printed Press. Articles have been published in Parenting Magazines and Parent Newsletters.
